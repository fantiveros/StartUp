What is iMEDitate?

iMEDitate is the easiest way to achieve a positive life. Giving you the power of mindfulness and relaxation in the palm of your hand, this app is a perfect adjunct to a mindfulness practice that will enable you to continue anytime, anywhere.

Target Users

iMeditate targets users who are stressed and burned out with self-meditating activities to reduce symptoms of much more than stress like depression and anxiety.
